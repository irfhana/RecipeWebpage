Authentic Smoked Prawn Delight

Dive into a world of exquisite culinary delight with our tantalizing smoked prawn recipe. This culinary masterpiece promises to elevate your seafood experience to new heights, blending succulent prawns with the mesmerizing allure of smoky goodness.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 50 minutes
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Large Prawns: Choose fresh, large prawns for a meaty and succulent texture.
  • 3 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Lemon Juice
  • 1 teaspoon Paprika
  • 1 teaspoon Smoked Paprika
  • 1 teaspoon Onion Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Dried Thyme
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Select wood chips or chunks compatible with your smoker (e.g., applewood, hickory, or mesquite) for imparting the desired smoky flavor.
  • Fresh Rosemary or Thyme sprigs for added aromatic infusion.
  • Chopped fresh Parsley or Cilantro for a burst of freshness.
  • Lemon wedges for serving
  • Fresh coriander or cilantro for garnish (optional)
  • Smoking box or aluminum foil pouch for wood chips.
  • Adjust the spice levels according to your preference, and you can also include additional ingredients like red food coloring or saffron for a more vibrant color.

Instructions

  • Clean and Prep Prawns:
    • Peel and devein the prawns, leaving the tails intact for presentation.

  • Prepare the Marinade:
    • In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, paprika, smoked paprika, onion powder, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.

  • Marinate Prawns:
    • Coat the prawns evenly with the marinade, ensuring each piece is well-covered. Allow them to marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to enhance flavor.

  • Soak Wood Chips:
    • If using wood chips, soak them in water for about 30 minutes to produce a steady, flavorful smoke.

  • Preheat Smoker:
    • Preheat your smoker to a temperature of around 225-250째F (107-121째C).

  • Add Wood Chips:
    • Place the soaked wood chips in the smoker box or create a foil pouch with holes to release the smoke. Add fresh herbs on top.

  • Smoke Prawns:
    • Arrange the marinated prawns on the smoker racks and smoke them for approximately 20-30 minutes, or until they reach an internal temperature of 145째F (63째C). The prawns should turn opaque and have a smoky aroma.

  • Garnish and Serve:
    • Remove the smoked prawns from the smoker, garnish with chopped parsley or cilantro, and serve with lemon wedges on the side.
