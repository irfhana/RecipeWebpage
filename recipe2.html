Authentic Chicken Tandoori Delight

Embark on a culinary journey with our Authentic Chicken Tandoori recipe, a feast for the senses that captures the essence of Indian flavors. Succulent pieces of chicken are marinated in a harmonious blend of yogurt and a medley of aromatic spices, creating a tantalizing symphony of taste and texture...

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs (about 1 kg) chicken pieces (drumsticks, thighs, or bone-in breasts)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon smoked or sweet paprika (for color)
  • 1 teaspoon ground fenugreek (kasuri methi) - optional
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Lemon wedges for serving
  • Fresh coriander or cilantro for garnish (optional)
  • Mint yogurt sauce for dipping (optional)
  • Adjust the spice levels according to your preference, and you can also include additional ingredients like red food coloring or saffron for a more vibrant color.

Instructions

  • Prepare the Chicken:
    • Clean and pat dry the chicken pieces. Make shallow cuts (slits) on the chicken to allow the marinade to penetrate.

  • Prepare the Marinade:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, garam masala, smoked paprika, ground fenugreek (if using), salt, lemon juice, and vegetable oil. Mix well to form a smooth and thick marinade.

  • Marinate the Chicken:
    • Coat the chicken pieces thoroughly with the marinade, ensuring each piece is well covered. For optimal flavor, let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2-4 hours or overnight.

  • Preheat the Oven or Grill:
    • If using an oven, preheat it to a high temperature (around 450째F/230째C). If using a grill, preheat it to medium-high.

  • Cook the Chicken:
    • Place the marinated chicken on a baking tray (lined with foil for easy cleanup) or on the grill.
    • Cook the chicken until it's fully cooked and has a charred, slightly crispy exterior. The internal temperature should reach 165째F (74째C).
    • Cooking times may vary, but it usually takes about 25-30 minutes in the oven or on the grill, turning the chicken halfway through.

  • Garnish and Serve:
    • Once cooked, garnish with fresh coriander or cilantro (optional) and serve hot with lemon wedges.
    • You can also serve it with mint yogurt sauce on the side for a refreshing contrast.
