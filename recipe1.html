Chicken Cheese Pasta

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with our delectable Creamy Chicken and Cheese Pasta. This enticing dish combines tender chunks of succulent chicken with al dente pasta, all generously coated in a luxuriously creamy cheese sauce. Every bite is a symphony of flavors, marrying the savory goodness of seasoned chicken with the rich, velvety texture of the cheese-infused sauce...

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 40 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (about 225g) of your preferred pasta (such as penne, fettuccine, or rotini)
  • 1 lb (about 450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup (about 240ml) heavy cream
  • 1 cup (about 100g) shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup (about 100g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)
  • Feel free to customize the recipe by adding vegetables like spinach, cherry tomatoes, or broccoli for extra flavor and nutrition.

Instructions

  • Cook the Pasta:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain the pasta and set aside.

  • Cook the Chicken:
    • Season the chicken pieces with salt, black pepper, dried oregano, and dried basil.
    • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
    • Add the seasoned chicken pieces and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from the skillet and set aside.

  • Prepare the Sauce:
    • In the same skillet, add minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
    • Pour in the heavy cream, stirring constantly, and bring it to a gentle simmer.

  • Add Cheese:
    • Reduce the heat to low and gradually stir in the shredded Parmesan and mozzarella cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.

  • Combine Pasta and Chicken:
    • Add the cooked pasta and chicken back into the skillet, tossing everything together until the pasta and chicken are evenly coated with the creamy cheese sauce.

  • Serve:
    • Taste and adjust seasoning if needed, adding more salt or pepper as desired.
    • Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired.
