Tropical Bliss Mixed Fruit Smoothie

Indulge your senses in a burst of freshness with our Tropical Bliss Mixed Fruit Smoothie. Crafted to perfection, this vibrant concoction is a celebration of nature's sweetness and vitality, blending an assortment of luscious fruits into a symphony of flavors that dance on your taste buds.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of your favorite liquid (water, coconut water, almond milk, or yogurt)
  • Ice cubes (optional for a colder smoothie)
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup diced mango
  • 1/2 cup diced pineapple
  • 1 medium banana, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced peaches (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup grapes (red or green), halved
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup for added sweetness
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Fresh mint leaves for a hint of freshness
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt or your favorite plant-based protein powder

Instructions

  • Prepare Fruits:
    • Wash, peel, and chop all fresh fruits as needed.

  • Blend:
    • In a blender, combine the mixed berries, diced mango, diced pineapple, sliced banana, sliced peaches, and halved grapes.

  • Liquid Base:
    • Pour in your chosen liquid base. Adjust the quantity based on your desired thickness for the smoothie.

  • Optional Add-ins:
    • If using, add the honey or maple syrup, vanilla extract, and any additional flavor boosters.

  • Protein Boost (Optional):
    • If you want to add protein, incorporate Greek yogurt or your preferred protein powder.

  • Blend Until Smooth:
    • Blend all the ingredients until smooth and creamy. If needed, you can add more liquid to reach your desired consistency.

  • Taste and Adjust:
    • Taste the smoothie and adjust sweetness or thickness by adding more sweetener or liquid, if desired.

  • Serve:
    • Pour the smoothie into glasses and garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.
